About Us

Ambush Gaming is aiming to be one of the best mobile gaming organizations in the world! Our team was founded off of friendship first.

We have been friends and competing in mobile games for nearly 6 years. Most of us starting our professional career from Rules of Survival. We have competed in the following: $20,000 Live me tournament and three $60,000.00 tournaments.

After rules of survival, our team decided to make our away into Call of Duty Mobile. We competed in Mobile Mayhem, but once call of duty mobile decided to take a different path, we did as well. Aftt Call of Duty Mobile, we decided to play Area F2 and Wild Rift.

After those 2 games, we decided to play Apex Mobile. Our team performed in the only 2 Mobile Mayhem events that Apex Mobile had.

We are now competing in Farlight 84 and we are the best team in North America by far. Ambush is dedicated on being the best organization in mobile gaming. We want to pick up the best teams while valuing friendship first. Remember, laughing with friends and winning tournaments is the best combination.
