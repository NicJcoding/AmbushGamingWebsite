About Us

Ambush Gaming is a competitive group of friends that plan to compete at the highest level in Mobile Gaming! Our team was founded off of friendship first.

We have been friends and competing in mobile games for nearly 6 years. Most of us starting our professional career from Rules of Survival. We have competed in the following: $10,000 Live me tournament and three $60,000.00 tournaments.

After rules of survival, our team decided to make our away into Call of Duty Mobile. We competed in Mobile Mayhem, but once call of duty mobile decided to take a different path, we did as well. After Call of Duty Mobile, we decided to play Area F2 and Wild Rift.

After those 2 games, we decided to play Apex Mobile. Our team performed in the only 2 Mobile Mayhem events that Apex Mobile had.

We are now competing in Farlight 84, where we have established our team as the best team in North America. We are also competiting in Rainbow Six Mobile! Ambush is dedicated on being the best team in all games. We want to pick up the best players while valuing friendship first. Remember, laughing with friends and winning tournaments is the best combination.
